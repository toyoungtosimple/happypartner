About Count Masters

Count Masters is an engaging stickman runner game where you control a crowd of characters and face off against opposing forces. Make smart choices through gates, grow your numbers, and defeat bosses in intense, fast-paced battles.

Key Features

  • Casual yet strategic stickman gameplay
  • Dynamic level design and obstacles
  • No downloads – play directly in your browser
  • Optimized for both desktop and mobile devices

How to Play

Use your mouse or touch gestures to guide your stickmen through number gates, increase your crowd, and dodge tricky traps. Conquer each level by reaching the end with the biggest group possible and defeating the boss!
